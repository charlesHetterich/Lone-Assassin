LONE ASSASSIN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
story:
Born into a clan of assassins, you are now at the age where you must prove yourself worthy to be an assassin yourself.
As is tradition, your clan members bring you into a random part of wilderness, and if you make it back home alive (across
the river) you wil have proven yourself worthy.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
objective:
reach the boat at the end of the (pretty long) path!
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
controls:
w-move up
s-move down
a-move right
d-move left
space bar-punch wood/animals/eat(while hovering over fire)
enter-create fire(requires 5 wood & 1 meat)
shift-sleep(useless)
